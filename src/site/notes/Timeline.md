### The Dawn of Time (13 billions years ago)

- The Celestials create the Universe
- Midi-chlorians are birthed from "The wellspring of Life" and creation of the force.
- The Son (Darkside), The Daughter (Light) and the Father (Balance) exist as powerful Force Wielders who live in the ethereal realm of Mortis.
- Billions of years before the imperial era, mineral matter that would infuse rock walls on the planet Ilum such as the Force-attuned kyber begin to accumulate.

- The Kwa come to existance native to the Outer Rim world of Dathomir.
- The Celestials taught the Kwa Force skills.
- The Kwa had advanced technologies over the galaxy such as the Hypergates and build the Infinity Gates and Star Temples.
- The Kwa ruled over many civilizations, one of them being the Rakata.
- The Kwa shared the Force skills and technologies with the Rakatan.
- Using the Dark side of the Force, the Rakatan rebelled and beat back the Kwa to their homeworld.
- In a last attempt to oppose the Rakatan, the Kwa built the Tho Yor and sent them into deep core. (For future purpose)

- (100 000 BBY) - Beggining of the construction of the first city levels of Coruscant in the Core Worlds 

### Dawn of the Jedi (36,453 - 25,000 BBY)

- (36,453 BBY) The Dai Bendu, a religious group that would existed as the precursor to the Jedi Order, discover the [[Tho Yor\|Tho Yor]] on Ando Prime. 
- The Tho Yor, weird alien-like pyramidal ships invited the Dai bendu Monks aboard and traveled into outer space, gathering many force-sensitive on many different worlds. 7 other Tho Yor did the same and brought them to the planet Tython.
- The selected Monkes named themself Je'daii, dedicating their lives to study the Tho Yor.

- (30,000 BBY) Humans of Coruscant had transformed the planet into a supermassive planetary city and already discovered interstellar travel.
- (27,500 BBY) Humans of Coruscant had started to quietly steal Rakatan technology. By this time, they were advanced enough to send out sleeper ships, colonizing other worlds and forming dozens of satellite civilisations. - See [[Coruscant planetary colonization\|Coruscant planetary colonization]].

- (26,000 BBY) The Je'daii studied the Force for over 10,000 years, studying both the Light (The Ashla) and the Dark (The Bogan), establishing temples, cities and places of learning.
- Due to the violent aspect of the planet, anyone who was not force sensitives had to leave, leading to the colonization of other planets in the Tythos system
- The Je'daii encouraged the usage of Light and dark side of the force, as it was necessary for the balance, not only the light like the modern Jedi.
- (25,800BBY) Overtime and because of a Rakatan invasion, members of the order began drifting towards one side or the other, leading to [[The Force War\|The Force War]].

- Discovery of the already-established Humans on Correllia by Coruscant
- (25,200BBY) -  The Rakatan empire, defeatd by war, rebellions and plagues, fled back to their homeworld, Lehon. 

### Before the Republic (25,200 BBY)

- With the Rakatan empire gone, all of the Civilizations began to expand and collide, either peacefully or with war. 
- They began to examine the far more advanced [[Rakatan technologies\|Rakatan technologies]] that they had left.
- (25,053 BBY) - Corellians and Duros independently reverse-engineered hyperspace cannons from the Rakatan [[Hyperdrive technology\|Hyperdrive technology]]. This discovery not only opened the worlds for trade, but also sent tremors throughout the Core, leading to the [[Unification War\|Unification War]].
- This Unification War consumed the Core and at the end it marked the establishment of the Galactic Republic
